Our Love Story

Our love story began in the most unconventional way, with a door swinging open and Andrea Storme exclaiming, “You will not believe what just happened!” That moment instantly intrigued Jordan, which sparked the beginning of many messages, voice calls, video calls and 5 seasons of Love is Blind. What started as curiosity quickly developed into something much deeper. Through every conversation, we built a bond that distance couldn’t break, and while the kilometers stretched between us, our hearts grew closer...and this blossomed into the beautiful love story we share now and which we can continue to add to.

Things became REAL when Jordan made the flight all the way from Scotland to South Africa…

And then another…

And then another from Storme’s side all the way to the UK for six months…

And then on the fourth trip, Jordan proposed!

We’ve faced our fair share of challenges, but every hurdle has only brought us closer and strengthened our bond. Despite the 10,337 kilometres that once separated us, we’ve overcome every obstacle. Now, hand in hand, we’re ready to travel the world that kept us apart, across seas, over mountains, and through valleys - forever adventurers in love.

Through all of this, you have been with us every step of the way, and we are so excited for you to share the most important day of our lives with us - our wedding.
